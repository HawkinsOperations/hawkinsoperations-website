Core thesis

AI is labor. Governance is authority.

AI can draft, compare, summarize, test candidate ideas, support implementation, and help reviewers find inconsistencies.

AI cannot promote truth, approve public claims, declare runtime state, or own evidence status. Human authorization controls final promotion.

Claim lifecycle

A public claim moves through gates.

  1. Source exists
  2. Static review
  3. Test defined
  4. Test validated
  5. Runtime active
  6. Signal observed
  7. Evidence linked
  8. Public safe

Truth boundaries

Separate claims before they become public proof.

01

Source truth

Files or artifacts exist.

02

Runtime truth

Systems are deployed, enabled, or running under stated conditions.

03

Signal truth

Telemetry, alerts, searches, or outputs were observed.

04

Evidence truth

Supporting material is preserved and linked.

05

Public proof

Wording is reviewed, scoped, approved, and safe for external use.

06

Legacy/reference truth

Historical material that does not become current truth by copy-paste.
